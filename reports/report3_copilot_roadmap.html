01
Current AI Trends in Digital Transformation
Four macro-trends reshaping enterprise digital transformation
Data Democratization & AI Readiness
Foundation for AI
AI models are only as good as the data they consume. This trend makes data an accessible, strategic asset for real-time, predictive decision-making and ethical AI deployment. Addresses the biggest roadblock to DT: organisations cannot leverage AI without clean, accessible data estates.
Legacy System Modernization with Gen AI
Overcoming Technical Debt
Addresses one of the biggest roadblocks to DT, allowing enterprises to create a modern, agile, and AI-ready core IT architecture. Moves organisations from managing technical debt to building a platform for AI-powered business outcomes.
Generative AI Agents
Process Reinvention
Moves DT beyond task automation to reinventing entire end-to-end business processes, driving massive gains in efficiency, quality, and speed. Agents coordinate across systems without human intervention, completing multi-step workflows autonomously.
AI-Powered Low Code / No Code
Citizen Development Velocity
Significantly accelerates solution development, empowers business users (citizen developers), and speeds up time-to-market for digital solutions. Lowers the barrier to enterprise AI application development beyond specialist engineering teams.
02
Current Copilot Trends in DT
Four forces shaping how Copilot is being deployed in enterprise digital transformation
Real-Time Access to Enterprise Systems
Opportunity: Data & Analytics
Copilots depend on access to good data, knowledge, connectors and semantic architecture. Real-time access to enterprise systems (ERP, CRM, PLM) and the ability to retrieve and reason over structured/unstructured data is the key differentiator between effective and ineffective Copilot deployments.
Governance, Trust, Ethics & Hybrid Deployments
Opportunity: Managed Services
As copilots handle sensitive tasks (finance, HR, supply chain), enterprises care about governance, compliance, model/data ownership, and deployment location (cloud vs on-prem vs hybrid). Some sectors refuse to deploy public AI — opening an opportunity for Fortude's governance and managed services practice.
Domain & Role-Specific Copilots
Vertical strength in Fashion, F&B, Mfg
General-purpose Copilot has made way for specialist versions tailored to functions or industries. Fortude's vertical depth in Fashion, F&B, Manufacturing, and Distribution is a natural differentiator — custom Copilots built on Infor M3 data are more valuable than generic solutions.
Copilot as Action / Workflow Engine
Opportunity: Automation & RPA
A shift from "give me an answer" to "take an action" is underway. Copilots are increasingly integrating into business workflows, triggering processes, connecting systems, and performing multi-step tasks — creating a natural extension point with Fortude's automation and RPA capabilities.
03
Tier 1 — Enable & Adopt
Lower effort, high-impact services to get clients started with Copilot and build early ROI evidence
Usage Analytics & Adoption Monitoring
Leverage built-in dashboards and metrics (e.g. usage of Copilot Chat, prompts, app breakdown) to understand which users/apps are engaging. Offer monthly adoption reports, help clients identify "Copilot champions" and usage gaps.
BenefitHelps quantify ROI and surface blockers early
Use Case Identification & Quick Wins
Work with clients to identify 3–5 quick-win scenarios where Copilot adds visible productivity value (meeting summary, email drafting, analysis in Excel). Run a short workshop (~2–4 weeks) to map business problems and deliver proof-of-value.
BenefitDemonstrates value early, builds stakeholder buy-in
Governance & Change Readiness
Ensure data access, security, roles & permissions, and usage policies are addressed before major rollout. Offer a governance assessment, policy-template library, readiness checklist for Copilot usage, and risk matrix.
BenefitReduces risk, enables smoother scale
04
Tier 2 — Expand & Embed
Services that go beyond "just using Copilot" to embedding it deeper into workflows and integrations
Connector & System Integration Services
Extend Copilot to access enterprise data, third-party systems, and knowledge bases (CRM, ERP, PLM). Build connectors, integrate Copilot into Outlook/Teams/CRM with clients' data, design RAG flows.
BenefitMakes Copilot more useful because it works with company-specific data, not just generic content
Role-Based Copilot Deployment
Deploy role-specific Copilot offerings (e.g. Copilot for Service, Copilot for Sales) tailored to client roles. Map role workflows, configure Copilot for that role, change-manage those user groups, measure impact.
BenefitAligns solution with business function and drives deeper adoption
Low-Code Workflow Automation + Copilot
Use low-code platforms (Power Automate, Power Apps) with Copilot to automate tasks: email drafting, case summaries, approval flows. Create automation templates using Copilot + low-code connectors for target processes.
BenefitReduces manual effort and fosters scale without custom code
Advanced Analytics & Insights-Driven Adoption
Use adoption data + Copilot results to continuously refine which use-cases succeed. Monthly insights review, highlight lagging roles/processes, recommend next wave of use-cases or training.
BenefitKeeps adoption momentum — moves from "pilot" to "scale"
05
Tier 3 — Transform & Customise
Higher effort, higher value services — building bespoke Copilot solutions and custom copilots for client-specific domains
Custom Copilot Creation (Copilot Studio / Azure OpenAI)
Build domain-specific copilots (e.g. "Merchandiser Copilot" for fashion, "Supplier-Exception Copilot" for manufacturing) using company data and business logic. Includes data ingestion, vector/embedding architecture, prompt design, custom UI/agent integration, rollout & monitoring.
BenefitDifferentiated IP, higher margin — helps clients transform rather than just adopt
Agent Orchestration & Digital-Worker Frameworks
Build multi-agent workflows where copilots coordinate tasks across systems (ERP, CRM, support desk) — agent triggers, alerts, summarisation, action. Architecture design, agent workflow library, integration with enterprise systems.
BenefitAutomates knowledge-work and scales transformation across the enterprise
Managed Services for Copilot Governance / MLOps
Provide ongoing monitoring, model governance, drift detection, usage cost control, and internal audit. Subscription service for "Copilot Operations" — monitor usage, cost, compliance; provide dashboards; remediate issues.
BenefitRecurring revenue and embeds Fortude as a strategic long-term partner
